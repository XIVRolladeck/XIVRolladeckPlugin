# XIV Rolladeck Plugin

**See who's spinning in Eorzea right now — and get there without ever leaving the game.**

XIV Rolladeck is a Dalamud plugin that brings the [XIV Rolladeck](https://xivrolladeck.com) DJ and venue directory directly into Final Fantasy XIV. Track your favourite DJs, browse open venues, and save housing addresses for one-click teleporting.

---

## Features

- **Live DJ feed** — see every DJ currently streaming, their viewer count, and which venue they're playing at
- **Live venue feed** — browse venues open right now, including event name and location
- **Track favourites** — star any DJ or venue to keep them pinned in your saved tab
- **Save addresses** — save a housing address directly from the plugin for quick in-game navigation
- **Status at a glance** — avatar borders show live status, saved status, and whether a DJ is playing at one of your saved venues
- **Two-column layout** — live and saved cards displayed side by side for easy browsing
- **Offline saved cards** — your tracked DJs and venues are still listed when they're not live

---

## Installation

XIV Rolladeck is distributed as a custom Dalamud plugin repository.

1. Open **Dalamud Settings** (`/xlsettings`)
2. Go to the **Experimental** tab
3. Under **Custom Plugin Repositories**, paste the following URL and hit **+**:

```
https://plugin.xivrolladeck.com/repo.json
```

4. Click **Save & Close**
5. Open the **Plugin Installer** (`/xlplugins`), search for **XIV Rolladeck**, and install

---

## Usage

Open the plugin window with `/rolladeck` or from the Dalamud plugin menu.

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **Live DJs** | All DJs currently streaming on Twitch |
| **Live Venues** | Venues open right now via event or Discord |
| **Saved DJs** | Your tracked DJs (live at top, offline below) |
| **Saved Venues** | Your tracked venues (live at top, offline below) |

**Starring** a DJ or venue (★ next to their name) adds them to your saved tabs.
**Save Address** copies the venue's housing location so you can teleport there in-game.

## For DJs & Venue Managers

The plugin pulls live data directly from XIV Rolladeck. To appear in the plugin, your DJ or venue profile needs to be listed on [xivrolladeck.com](https://xivrolladeck.com). Listings are free.
